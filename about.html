Our original founders are college dropouts turned into millionaires, by the name of Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak. Their company brought new vision to the way people saw computers. The Apple computer revolutionized the industry, being the first to use color graphics. Now, we are a $733 billion company, outranking the entire Gross Domestic Product of Switzerland and the entire Spanish stock market, selling on an international scale.
