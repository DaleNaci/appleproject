The iPod

iPod Classic

Release Date: September 5, 2007
The original iPod was a simple but revolutionary handheld device. The slogan "1000 songs in your pocket" entices millions of people all throughout the entire globe to buy into our product. It comes with a simple click wheel to go through songs, selecting one at your liking.

iPod Shuffle

Release Date: September 1, 2010
The Fourth Generation iPod Shuffle features an extremely basic way to take around music and instantly put it on shuffle. It is "jaw-droppingly small" and is the first waterproof iPod. It now features a voiceOver in 29 different languages, including Czech, Danish, Slovak, and more. It now comes in seven colors: black, green, blue, pink, yellow, purple, or red.

iPod Nano

Release Date: September 12, 2012
The Ipod Nano is a state-of-the-art MP3 player. The storage in this product can be bought with either 8gB or 16GB of memory, and comes in 9 colors: silver, grey, purple, blue, green, yellow, orange, red, and pink. Installed into the hardrive is an FM Radio, along with VoiceOver, Fitness pedometer, and a Video Camera.

iPod Touch

Release Date: September 12, 2012
The iPod Touch revolutionizes the mobile industry with simple but extremely useful concepts. This includes the obvious touch screen capability, along with App Stores, Safari, and YouTube connections. It can record 1080p videos, and comes in five colors: silver, pink, yellow, blue, and red.
